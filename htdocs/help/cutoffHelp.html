Conditions to exclude low quality sequences
Conditions to exclude low quality bisulfite sequences can be changed. These parameters can also be changed after data submission.
  Upper limit of unconverted CpHs
number of unconverted CpHs (CpA, CpC and CpT)
(parameter to exclude incomplete bisulfite conversion)
  Lower limit of percent converted CpHs
percent of  "number of converted CpHs" / "number of CpHs"
(parameter to exclude incomplete bisulfite conversion)
  Upper limit of alignment mismatches
number of alignment mismatches and gaps between genomic and bisulfite sequences
(parameter to exclude low quality sequence read)
  Lower limit of percent identity
percent of alignment identity between genomic and bisulfite sequences
(parameter to exclude raw quality sequence read)

Zipped archive of sequence files
Zipped archives that consist of a single folder containing bisulfite sequence files in FASTA, plain sequence or ABI (electropherogram) format can be uploaded. Acceptable file extensions of sequence files are ".seq", ".fa", ".fas", ".fasta", ".ab1" or ".txt".

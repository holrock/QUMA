  • Bisulfite sequencing analysis tools

    • MethylMapper: High-throuput mapping tool (command line)

  • BiSearch: Primer Design and Search Tool
  • Methylator: An SVM Based Method for DNA Methylation Prediction
  • MethMarker: MethMarker facilitates the design and optimization of DNA methylation assays
